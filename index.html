    About Us

    ROBONAUTS

    The IIITN Robotics Team is an entirely student-run group of highly motivated undergraduate students from the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Nagpur. We an organisation that helps students foster their interest towards different domains of robotics. Our main motto is to provide students a workplace and a healthy environment to pursue their interest in mechanical, electronic and software design . We here are dedicated to building cool robots and learning new things. We seek to provide IIITN undergraduates with not only the opportunity to participate in exciting and educational robotics projects, but also to build a warm and accomplished community of engineers together.
